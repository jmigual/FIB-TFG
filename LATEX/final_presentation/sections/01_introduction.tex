\subsection{Survival Analysis}
\begin{frame}{\insertsubsec}
  Survival analysis models usually have:
  \begin{itemize}
    \item Baseline data \( x \)
    \item Event \( E \in \{0, 1\} \)
    \item Time \( T \)
  \end{itemize}
  
  Survival function:
  \[
    S(t) = \Pr(T \ge t)
  \]

  Hazard function:
  \[
    \lambda(t) = \lim_{\Delta t \rightarrow 0}
    \frac{\Pr(t \le T < t + \Delta t | T \ge t)}{\Delta t}
  \]
\end{frame}
